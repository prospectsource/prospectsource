Servicing College Basketball Prospects Servicing College Basketball Programs Contact Contact forum for questions LANDING PAGE SECTIONS SERVICING COLLEGE BASKETBALL PROSPECTS AAU Prospects Our service is built to help AAU athletes through the recruiting process. We believe the recruiting process is complex and it is ultimately up to the athlete to perform on and off the court in order to have the opportunity to play college basketball. We service our athletes all year round as we prepare them for college basketball. During the fall and winter months we want our athletes utilizing all the resources offered at their respective high school. During the spring and summer, we offer additional resources through their respective AAU program to make sure athletes are doing everything possible to reach their goals. For many this simply means education on the process and information dissemination. For other athletes, this is a more in depth development opportunity as we help our athletes become physically and mentally prepared to perform.
