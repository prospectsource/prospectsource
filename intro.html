WHAT WE DO

Prospect Source is a recruiting service built to help athletes through the recruiting process. We believe the recruiting process is complex and it is ultimately up to the athlete to perform on and off the court in order to have the opportunity to play college basketball. We believe it is our job to help educate and train athletes on this process, while offering resources. Our end goal is to empower both athletes and college basketball coaching staffs.

COLLEGE BASKETBALL PROSPECTS

Our service is built to help AAU athletes through the recruiting process. We believe the recruiting process is complex and it is ultimately up to the athlete to perform on and off the court in order to have the opportunity to play college basketball. We service our athletes all year round as we prepare them for college basketball.

During the fall and winter months we want our athletes utilizing all the resources offered at their respective high school. During the spring and summer, we offer additional resources through their respective AAU program to make sure athletes are doing everything possible to reach their goals. For many this simply means education on the process and information dissemination. For other athletes, this is a more in depth development opportunity as we help our athletes become physically and mentally prepared to perform.

Service Overview:

  • Basic Service

    Register on our platform to receive an evaluation and gain access to resources.

  • Video Service

    Let us work with your video to create highlights and to share game film with college coaches for scouting.

  • Marketing Service

    Do you have highlight videos, game film, or Hudl video? We want to make sure relevant coaches across the country have your content.

  • Consulting Service

    Work with our consultants to make sure your physical skills, mentality, and opportunity put you in the best position to reach your goals of playing college basketball.

COLLEGE BASKETBALL PROGRAMS

NCAA (Division I, II, III), NAIA, NJCAA

Our goal is to help college basketball programs of all levels improve their prospect discovery process. We make sure college coaches have the necessary information, video, and relationships to easily identify relevant prospects at AAU Programs across the country. We understand college coaches cannot see every athlete live, therefore we work to make sure they have a resource to identify prospects who would be a good fit for their program.
