OUR SERVICE

We help college coaches gather information on prospects across the United States. Through our relationships with various clubs, coaches, and schools, we make sure college coaches have the necessary tools to connect with and discover new prospects. Our goal is to provide each college coaching staff with as much information on prospects as possible in order to evaluate and improve the prospect discovery process. We want to give each staff a competitive edge by providing new reports and more in-depth information about the recruitment of each prospect in our database.

COLLEGE COACH DISCOVERY PLATFORM

  • Access Database

    Connect with our member clubs so you can learn more about their prospects.

  • Filter Reports

    Create reports to filter prospects according to specific athletic, academic, or recruiting data.

  • Discover Prospects

    Access our recruiting feeds to see when an athlete at your level is recruited.

  • Track Recruiting

    Keep tabs on prospects you are interested in by seeing complete transparency in their recruitment.

  • Receive Video

    Let us know if you would like to obtain video of a prospect at one of our member clubs for scouting purposes.
